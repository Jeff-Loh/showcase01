Chef Lite(About Us)

ChefLite is an ingredient-and-recipe meal kit service that sends pre-portioned and partially-prepared food ingredients right to your doorstep to prepare homecooked meals. We want our customers to experience food from all different cultures which are why new recipes will be added each month for you to experience each culture's cuisines. Our motto: 'Discovering the Chef in Us' perfectly describes our objective, which is to help you improve on your cooking skills with the use of the ingredients provided to you within each meal kit. We aim to get you to be more active within the kitchen while also helping you satisfy your hunger!
